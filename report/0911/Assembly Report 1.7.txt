 1). In an 8-bit binary number, which is the most significant bit (MSB)? 
Most significant bit (the highest numbered bit.)
Leftmost bit
The most significant bit (MSB) in an 8-bit binary number is the leftmost bit, which represents the highest power of 2, specifically 2^7 (or 128). It plays a key role in determining the sign of numbers in certain binary representations.
